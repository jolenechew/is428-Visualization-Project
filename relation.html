Relation between Earthquakes and Tsunamis

A deeper analysis of these disasters, how they relate, and how they aggravate each other.




Let's first see how often Earthquakes and Tsunamis co-occur, and what are some attributes an Earthquake has that makes it strong enough to cause a Tsunami.






A closer look at the relation between an earthquake's magnitude and the effects it has on the attributes of a tsunami:






When we move around the axis (the different attributes), we observe an overall positive correlation between an earthquake's magnitude and the number of run ups and tsunami intensity of the corresponding tsunami. However, the correlation might not be as strong for the maximum water height and the number of deaths.
