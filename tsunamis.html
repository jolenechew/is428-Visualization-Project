Tsunamis

An overview of tsunamis and run-ups that occur after.




Overview of Tsunamis since the 1900s









The Ring of Fire circles the Pacific. It hosts most of the planets earthquakes and volcanoes. JAPAN, USA, and INDONESIA are countries located on the Ring of Fire and are the top 3 countries most prone to tsunamis. Tsunamis also have a variety of causes, with the top cause being Earthquakes. We can also observe a slight relation between tsunami intensity and the total deaths, where a higher tsunami intensity brought about more deaths in several occasions.




Looking at the Run-ups caused by Tsunamis







Run-ups occur when a tsunami floods the coastal areas, causing water levels to rise and cover land. The height of the run-up often varies depending on the size of the tsunami and the shape of the coastline.
